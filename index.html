about

hi! my name is nuha 👋🏽

I am a junior studying Computer Science and minoring in Information Systems at University of Maryland, Baltimore County. I am also a teaching fellow for UMBC’s Computing Success Center and a research assistant at the Bodies in Motion Lab. My interests include art, design, and the connection between humans and technology. Upon graduation, I plan to explore this further by pursuing a Master's in Human-Centered Computing.

Currently, I am seeking opportunities to develop new technical skills, network with others in my field, and challenge myself!
